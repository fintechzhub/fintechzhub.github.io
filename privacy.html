Privacy Policy

Welcome to FinTechz Hub. We respect your privacy and want you to know exactly how we handle information. Since our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages, we do not have a database to store your personal information like names, phone numbers, or addresses.

1. Data Collection

No Personal Data: We do not ask for or collect any personal data unless you contact us directly via email.

Log Files: Like most websites, we use log files which include IP addresses, browser types, and timestamps. This is handled by the hosting server (GitHub) to help us understand site traffic.

Calculators: Our EMI, FD, and RD calculators run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No data you enter into these tools is ever saved or sent to any server.

2. External Links

Our site contains links to third-party websites (like 'Apply Now' buttons for credit cards). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.
